Chicken Tinola

Description

Tinola is an easy, one-pot soup from the Philippines. Chicken, chayote squash (or papaya!), bok choy, and spinach are simmered with fresh ginger in a savory broth that's often served with white rice. Taken from allrecipes

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil
  • Onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Thinly sliced ginger
  • Tablespoon Fish sauce
  • Chicken thighs, bone-in, deskinned
  • Chicken broth
  • Chayote
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Baby bok choy
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large pot. Add onion, garlic, and ginger. Stir fry for a few minutes
  2. Add fish sauce. Add chicken and cook for a few minutes until slightly cooked
  3. Add chicken broth, season with salt and pepper. Cook until no pink in chicken
  4. Add bok choy and cook for a few minutes.
