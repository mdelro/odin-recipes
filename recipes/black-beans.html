Black Beans

Description

This recipe is for Cuban style black beans that is best eaten with rice and or fried plantains.

Ingredients

  • Goya Canned Black Beans
  • Water
  • Minced Garlic
  • Recaito
  • Red Cooking Wine
  • Vinegar
  • White Sazon
  • Adobo
  • Bay leaves
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Open canned black beans and pour into pot
  2. Fill the can with water about above half-way the can
  3. Pour water in the pot
  4. Add a big spoonful of minced garlic, recaito, and 4 olives
  5. Add white sazon packet and a few shakes of adobo
  6. Pour about 1/4 cup of red cooking wine
  7. Add a splash of white vinegar
  8. Add bay leaves
  9. Stir with a spoon and then cover
  10. Bring to a simmer and cook for about 20 minutes, or until the liquid is slightly reduced
  11. Add water as needed if water gets too low
